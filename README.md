## What it does

Paste any suspicious email (sender, subject, body) and the AI will instantly:

- Classify it as **Phishing**, **Suspicious**, or **Legitimate**
- Give a **confidence score** and **risk score out of 100**
- List all **red flags** detected in the email
- List **safe signals** that suggest legitimacy
- Provide **actionable tips** on what to do next

## How to use

1. Clone or download this repo
2. Open `phishing-detector.html` in your browser
3. Enter the sender address, subject line, and email body
4. Click **Analyze email**
5. Get your AI verdict instantly

> Tip: You can also press `Ctrl + Enter` (or `Cmd + Enter` on Mac) to analyze quickly.
